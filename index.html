VarnishCalc

A professional coating calculator designed for internal coating applications. It translates process inputs into clear, actionable coating values — reducing manual calculations and improving consistency.

What it does: Calculates coating weight and thickness based on process parameters.
Focus: Internal coatings for aluminum packaging.
Output: Dry & wet layer weight (g/can, g/m²) and thickness (µm).
